Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Himpri

How do I join a contest on Himpri?
Joining a contest is simple! First, create your account, then browse through available contests. Select one that interests you, pay the entry fee (if applicable), and you're ready to participate at the scheduled time.
How do I withdraw my winnings?
You can withdraw your winnings through various methods including UPI, Bank Transfer, and digital wallets. Go to the 'My Transactions' section, click on 'Withdraw', enter the amount and your preferred withdrawal method. Most withdrawals are processed within 24 hours.
Are there any free contests?
Yes! We regularly host free contests with smaller prize pools. These are great for newcomers to get familiar with how our platform works without any financial commitment.
What happens if I disconnect during a contest?
Our system is designed to handle temporary disconnections. If you get disconnected, you can rejoin the contest as long as it's still ongoing. Your progress will be saved for the questions you've already answered.
